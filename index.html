Google has juste given some new details about its upcoming game streaming platform Stadia. Among other details, it announced that a Stadia Pro subscription would cost only 9.99$ USD per month, while a preorder of its Founder Edition subscription cost just 129.99$ USD. This Founded Edition subscription bundle together:

  1. A 3 months Stadia Pro subscription
  2. A Google controller
  3. A Google Chromecast Pro
